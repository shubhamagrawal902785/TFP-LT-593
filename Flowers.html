FLOWERS: MEANING AND SYMBOLISM




Flowers, a common item in our daily life seen from our houses to offices, used from birthdays to funerals. But do we know about flowers?
Nature is seen as beauty and people are heavily influenced by it's emotions. Flower meanings allow a tangible exchange of these emotions. It seems like a common knowledge that roses symbolise love, but you have ever wondered or been curious about the meanings behind other flowers? Do you know that rose meanings include more than just love and romance ? No? So let's explore what some of the most popular flowers mean.
