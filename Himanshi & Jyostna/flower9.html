Lilac

The word lilac refers to the light purple colour of it's flowers.
The meaning of the lilac flower has diversified throughout the ages.
Because lilacs have one of the earliest bloom times, they symbolise confidence, which makes them a traditionally a popular gift for graduates. There are many meanings that lilacs have :

  • White lilacs symbolise purity and innocence.
  • Violet lilacs symbolise spirituality.
  • Blue lilacs symbolise happiness and tranquility.
  • Magenta lilacs symbolise love and passion.
  • Lilacs, the colour for which this flower is named, is a light purple that symbolises a first love.
