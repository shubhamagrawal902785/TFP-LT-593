Aster

Aster is a unique flower with a star shaped flower head. According to the greek mythology, aster was created when the tears of goddess Astraea fell to the ground when she was crying because she was upset with the less stars present in the sky.

Asters mainly represent love, wisdom and faith.

The flower carries a variety of meaning with a variety of colours.

  • Purple aster symbolise wisdom and royalty.
  • White aster symbolise purity and innocence.
  • Red aster symbolise undying devotion.
  • Pink aster symbolise sensitivity and love.
